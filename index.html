The Foundation

Awareness Is a Competitive Edge

Awareness of strengths and weaknesses is vital to building a successful team. Understanding each team member's objective strengths and weaknesses, what motivates them, and their preferred work and communication styles allows the team to adapt its behaviors and strategies to achieve at a higher level.

Utilizing the Predictive Index, the Auburn University Gymnastics Program, guided by an Advisor from AUDIA Consulting, will thoroughly explore individual and team dynamics. Through rigorous, objective data analysis, we aim to elevate self-awareness and foster a more profound understanding among team members, leading to greater trust.

This strategic initiative will elevate productivity and performance, promoting unity, collaboration, and shared success within the Auburn Gymnastics family: on the floor, in the gym, and beyond.

The Predictive Index®
Building Stronger Teams. Together.

The Predictive Index (PI) is a talent optimization platform that helps us understand what drives people, how they work best, and how they can work better together. Built on more than 60 years of behavioral science, PI translates how each person on your roster naturally operates, and how to harness those differences for championship-level performance.
